Some Nirvana history:

  • 1987 - This legendary rock band was formed in Aberdeen,Washington
    by lead singer and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic
  • 1990 - Drummer Dave Grohl joined Nirvana.
  • 1991 - Their world-wide succes begun with song "Smells Like A Teen Spirit"from their 2nd album "Nevermind".
  • 1993 - Nirvana released their abrasive and less mainstream sounding third studio album, "In Utero".
  • 1994 - Nirvana felt apart following Cobain's death in April 1994. His widow is an American singer, songwriter and actress, Courtney Love.
  • 2004 - Rolling Stone named Nirvana among the greatest artists of all time.
  • '87-2009 - They had sold over 75 million records worldwide, making them one of the best-selling bands of all time.
  • 2014 - They were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in their first year of eligibility.
